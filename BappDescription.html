Autowasp is a Burp Suite extension that integrates OWASP Web Security Testing Guide (WSTG) directly into your testing workflow. It provides a structured environment for penetration testers to track progress, log traffic, and generate comprehensive reports aligned with industry standards.

Key Features:

  • Full WSTG Integration: Access the complete OWASP WSTG checklist with objectives and reference links.
  • Offline Mode: Loaded with a bundled version of the WSTG checklist for environments without internet access.
  • Intelligent Logger: Capture and consolidate issues from Burp Scanner, Proxy, Repeater, and Intruder.
  • Automated Mapping: Map findings and traffic instances directly to WSTG test cases.
  • Evidence Collection: Store vulnerable request/response pairs and detailed observations for each finding.
  • Advanced Memory Management: Optimized for large projects with pagination and active memory monitoring to ensure stability.
  • Professional Reporting: Export your progress and findings into structured Excel reports ready for client delivery.

The extension is designed for both beginners seeking guidance and professionals looking for a systematic way to manage their testing activities.
